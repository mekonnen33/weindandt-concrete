Who We Are

A Concrete Stamp That Means Something

Kyle Weinandt started this company with one idea: do the job right the first time, every time. Concrete is permanent — it either looks great for decades or it doesn't. There's no middle ground, and there's no hiding bad work.

We've been doing this in the Twin Cities since before most guys had a website. Every job gets the same treatment whether it's a $2,000 sidewalk repair or a $30,000 stamped patio — proper base prep, right mix, right finish, right seal.

The W Stamp Concrete is permanent — it leaves a mark. So does the quality of the work. Every project gets the W stamp because we stand behind every pour we make.
